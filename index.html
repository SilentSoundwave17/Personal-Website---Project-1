    Jimmy's Feed Co.
    Mural


    A locally-run sandwich store in Waterloo comissioned me to design and paint a mural on a wall inside of their store.

    The owner, Jimmy Boone, gave me full creative liberties on the content of the mural, with a few specific additions he wanted added.


    The mural is a depiction of well-known celebrities, products, and landmarks from the Kitchener, Waterloo and Cambridge regions.

    I first created a digital prototype of the mural before getting approval to transfer the mural to the wall.
